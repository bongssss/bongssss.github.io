Welcome to Beth-Gilgal International School



Located in Shelter Afrique, Uyo, Akwa-Ibom state ​​; Gilgal Internatioanal School is a purpose driven school equipped with state of the art 21st century school architecture in a well situated and secure environment. The name of the school is a reflection of its mandate taken from 'Joshua 5:9'.

MISSION

TO RAISE GODLY GENERATIONAL LEADERS WITH PASSION FOR EXCELLENCE, HARD WORK AND THE FEAR OF GOD.

VISION

TO BE A LEADING SCHOOL IN EARLY CHILD CHARACTER FORMATION FOR LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT.

MOTTO

PURSUIT OF EXCELLENCE THROUGH INTERGRITY AND HARD WORK.

GILGAL REPRESENTS  A PLACE WHERE GOD ROLLED AWAY THE REPROACH OF EGYPT FROM THE JEWS(WE ARE RAISING A GENERATION THAT WILL REDEEM OUR NATION FROM THE PRESENT HOPLESSNESS AND DECAY)

GILGAL REPRESENTS A PLACE WHERE KINGS WERE CORONATED AND THE TIME  IS NOW TO CORONATE OUR FUTURE LEADERS.


 AT GILGAL INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL WE ARE COMMITED TO RAISING GENERATIONAL LEADERS THROUGH THE PURSUIT OF EXCELLENCE, INTEGRITY, HARD-WORK AND THE FEAR OF GOD BECAUSE WE BELIEVE ;

'THE BEST WAY TO PREDICT THE FUTURE IS TO CREATE IT'
  ---PETER DUCKER .
